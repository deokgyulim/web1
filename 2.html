1. Introduction of Company for Case Study: McDonald’s

1) Founded and initial successful growth

Mcdonald's is Worldwide spread fast food franchise that has about 80 years of history. They founded in 1940 in California, US. The name McDonald’s came from the original restaurant founders who is brother “Maurice McDonald and Richard McDonald”. McDonald’s restaurant had a good tasty foods and services. But Restaurant McDonald’s could not have achieved the successful growth, if had not met “Ray Kroc” who is the Service and business developer. “Ray Kroc” who was chairman of company McDonald’s have started franchising in 1955. He created standard-produced fast food system that led to McDonald's initial rapid growth.

2) Situation of McDonald‘s in South KOREA

McDonald's opened South Korea's first McDonald's franchise restaurant in Seoul in March 1988. Their initial expansion of franchise stores was sluggish. but in the 2000s, with successful marketing and customer’s positive response, they recently have about 450 above stores in South Korea. On the other hand, recently it is known that its competitors' aggressive marketing and social issues threaten sales.
